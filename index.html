About Me

Hi! I'm a doctoral student in the ex-situ lab at Université Paris-Saclay. My thesis work explores how to design interactive player histories in persistent game worlds. Together with my brother, I develop We Ride, a massively multiplayer online role-playing game which serves as a rich and unique testbed for conducting research. I consider myself incredibly fortunate in being able to pursue my passion for game design through research.

I graduated from KTH and EIT Digital in 2018 with a joint masters degree in media technology and innovation & entrepreneurship. While studying, I also had the great opportunities to launch two startups, participate in exchange programs to Hong Kong, Paris and Berlin, and engage in exciting student chapter projects.
